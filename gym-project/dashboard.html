Cardio Guide

Cardio exercises are essential for improving cardiovascular health. Here are some exercises you can try:

  • Running
  • Cycling
  • Jumping Rope
  • Swimming
  • High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)
Core Guide

Core exercises help strengthen your abdominal muscles and improve stability. Here are some exercises you can try:

  • Planks
  • Russian Twists
  • Bicycle Crunches
  • Leg Raises
  • Mountain Climbers
Stamina Guide

Stamina exercises help improve your endurance and overall fitness. Here are some exercises you can try:

  • Long-Distance Running
  • Rowing
  • Elliptical Training
  • Stair Climbing
  • Burpees
